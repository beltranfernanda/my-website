My journey

Since childhood, I've been fascinated by computers. My early days were spent playing online games, but a pivotal moment came when I was 8. My aunt and uncle moved abroad, leaving me as the only person at home who knew how to communicate grandma with them through Messenger. I also became the go-to person for using our home computer. This marked the beginning of my journey as a computer enthusiast. I started to provide support for our family computer and internet network, laying the foundation for my passion for technology.


After graduating from high school, I was passionate about pursuing a career in computer engineering. I applied and was admitted to a prestigious 3-year engineering program at one of the best universities in my city. My journey into the tech world began in earnest in 2019 with an internship in QA. Six months later, the company recognized my potential and offered me a full-time contract as a software developer through a bootcamp program. Over the next two years, I honed my skills as a full-stack developer, working with technologies like Java, JavaScript, Liferay, CSS, HTML, and Spring MVC.


In 2021, an opportunity with one of Latin America's leading e-commerce companies led me to work as a software developer, where I expanded my tech stack to include Golang, React, Node.js, Spring Boot, Kibana, and New Relic, among others.
